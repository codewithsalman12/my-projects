FASQ

What is your return policy?

At PETBOT, you can return any product within 7 days if it’s damaged or incorrect. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Contact us with your order details, and we ll arrange a replacement or refund.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, PETBOT ships products internationally. Shipping charges and delivery times may vary based on your location. We ll share tracking details once your order is shipped. Please contact us for specific country availability or shipping estimates.

How can I track my order?

OOnce your order is shipped, PETBOT will send you a tracking link on your email or WhatsApp. Click the link to see the live status of your delivery. If you didn’t receive it, please contact our support with your order number.
